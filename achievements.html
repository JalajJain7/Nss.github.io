Achievements

NSS BIT MESRA, as a distinguished body, upholding the mission of social service and public welfare, has been an epitome of selfless contributions. With a keen, pulsating desire that transcends differences of all sorts, our members, be it Volunteers, Executives, Program-Coordinators, Program-Officers have achieved milestones and have collectively constructed NSS BIT MESRA as a vibrant space, garnering universal appreciation.

Some of our significant achievements are listed below -

  • Our two volunteers Mr. Prashant Kumar & Mr. Ravi Kumar got NSS award by The President of India for their outstanding contribution to the society.
  • Our program officer Dr. Om Prakash Pandey got the NSS AWARD for the year (2018-19) by the President of India for their outstanding contribution to literate the poor people of villages ad provide all the necessary resources.
  • We secured 4th position in India to make our campus cashless under Digital India Mission for which our four volunteers Mr. Prashant Kumar, Mr. Nitin Agarwal, Ms Shalini Lata and Mr. Ravi Kumar got the best volunteers award by the MHRD at Vigyan bhawan, New Delhi.
  • Mr. Rahul Raj ex - president received NSS AWARD for the year (2019 - 20) by the President of India.
