Independence Day

Prabhat Pheri

National Service Scheme (NSS), BIT Mesra starts the Independence Day celebrations with the 'Prabhat Pheri', which is one of the ethnic Indian ways to promote fitness and health. Hundreds of enthusiastic students, teachers, and staff members, stride the college on foot and recite patriotic slogans in chorus with eagerness. This spreads the benefits of fitness which will bring in a positive change in society.
