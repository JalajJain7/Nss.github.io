Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav

Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav

Mr. Aditya Ranjan
|
CEO JSLPS

On the 2nd of April, the programme was inaugurated with an engrossing webinar the guest speaker of which was Mr. Aditya Ranjan, CEO JSLPS, who enlightened the audience on the progress the country had made so far. With an attendance of 120+ listeners, the programme, held on Webex Meet was an interactive one with question answer session. The audience got insights into the government system and were also guided on how to be a good citizen and help the country to prosper.
